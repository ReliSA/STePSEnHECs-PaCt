# Keep Team Spirit (Draft)

## Context

## Problem

The end of the assignment is in sight, but the motivation and inspiration starts to decrease.

## Forces

## Solution

Don’t work too long and make regularly breaks. Finish the assignment together as group with something nice you can look forward to, like going out for a beer.
